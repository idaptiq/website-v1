About Idaptiq

We're automating the heaviest parts of clinical trial delivery.

Trials are slowed by work that repeats study after study — SAP authoring, dataset specifications, analysis code, and validation. Idaptiq turns that work into automation that biostats teams can trust.

The product

Idaptiq is an automation platform purpose-built for biostatistics and statistical programming teams. It encodes the domain logic behind modern clinical trials — SAPs, SDTM and ADaM dataset specifications, TLF programming, and aCRF workflows — as modular, reusable skills.

Those skills power an engine that generates specifications, code, and analysis outputs together, with independent validation and traceability built in. The result is a pipeline that shortens timelines while holding the line on the regulatory rigor sponsors and regulators expect.

What it covers today

  • Statistical analysis plan authoring and structured logic
  • SDTM and ADaM dataset specifications
  • TLF programming and analysis outputs
  • Independent validation workflows and cross-language code replication
  • Pooled analyses across studies and aCRF automation

Who it's for

Sponsors and CROs running clinical trials that need to move faster without compromising on CDISC compliance, validation, or regulatory credibility.

The team

Idaptiq is built by biostatistics SMEs, senior statistical programmers, and software engineers who have spent their careers inside clinical trial delivery.
