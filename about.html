About idaptiq

Built for biostatistics, by biostatistics.

Trials are slowed by work that repeats study after study — SAP authoring, dataset specifications, analysis code, and validation. idaptiq turns that work into AI-driven automation that biostats teams can trust.

The product

idaptiq is the AI platform for the full clinical trial biostatistics lifecycle. It automates 1,000+ hours of manual work per study and intelligently connects the protocol with every downstream idaptiq-generated file — creating an integrated end-to-end linkage system that syncs through iterations and amendments.

The platform spans five integrated modules — Foundation, SAP, SDTM, ADaM, and TLFs — linked by a shared knowledge graph. Outputs are generated, reasoned about, and validated by idaptiq's AI agent in the cloud, while raw patient data stays inside the customer's VPC or on-prem environment.

What makes idaptiq different

  • Deeply interconnected — metadata within and between all modules connects and updates downstream and upstream files, minimizing rework and reliance on intensive labor and memory.
  • Amendment-aware — unplanned changes to protocols, SAPs, CRFs, or EDC propagate through every dependent artifact.
  • Embedded institutional knowledge — the system understands context, dependencies, and interdependent relationships, supporting statistical teams and mitigating risks associated with resourcing shifts.
  • Traceability enabled — generated files are fully auditable with machine-readable lineage, satisfying FDA and EMA submission standards.

Who it's for

Sponsors and CROs running clinical trials that need to move faster without compromising on CDISC compliance, validation, or regulatory credibility.
